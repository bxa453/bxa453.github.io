There are several different Military branches in Mexico. Some are Army (Ejercito), Mexican Air Force (Fuerza Aerea Mexicana, FAM), Mexican Navy (Armada de Mexico (ARM) and Air Force (Fuerza Aerea Mexicana,FAM).

Communications

Broadcast Media

Mexico has 821 TV stations and 1,745 radio stations. The Televisa group once had a virtual monopoly in TV broadcasting. Mexico was the first country in Latin America to transition from analog to digital. This would let the viewers have better image quality and better audio quality.

Internet Country Code

Mexico's country code is .mx.

Internet Users
